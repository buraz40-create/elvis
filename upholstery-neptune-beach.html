There's something quietly special about Neptune Beach. Tucked between Atlantic Beach and Jacksonville Beach, it has a personality all its own — unhurried, unpretentious, and deeply residential. The homes here tend toward the well-considered rather than the ostentatious: quality over show, warmth over flash. It's a community where handcrafted upholstery and carefully chosen drapery aren't luxuries — they're simply part of living well. And that's precisely the philosophy that has guided Elvis Upholstery & Draperies since we opened our Atlantic Beach showroom in 2007.

Neptune Beach homeowners are some of our most discerning clients. They come to us with a clear sense of what they want — or a clear sense that they need expert help to find it — and they leave with custom pieces that are genuinely personal. We've upholstered heirloom armchairs passed down through families, built custom headboards that transformed master bedrooms, and dressed windows with draperies that have become the defining element of their rooms. Neptune Beach deserves that level of care, and that's what we deliver.

The Neptune Beach Aesthetic & Our Approach

Neptune Beach homes often feature a natural, organic palette — whites, creams, soft blues, warm taupes, and the natural tones of wood, rattan, and linen. They're designed to feel like an extension of the coastal environment just steps away, with interiors that breathe and feel lived-in rather than staged.

Our fabric selection serves this aesthetic beautifully. Belgian linens in warm neutrals, performance velvets in muted jewel tones, natural weaves and bouclés, and Sunbrella fabrics in coastal colorways — all available in our Atlantic Beach showroom, just minutes from Neptune Beach. Our team understands the Neptune Beach palette intuitively, and we'll help you find the fabric that feels exactly right.

We also understand the practical realities of coastal living: the humidity that can degrade inferior foam, the UV exposure that can fade unprotected fabrics, the salt air that requires corrosion-resistant hardware. Every specification we make for Neptune Beach clients accounts for these factors — because a beautiful piece that fails in two years is no investment at all.

Heirloom & Antique Upholstery in Neptune Beach

Neptune Beach's established residential character means we work frequently with heirloom and antique pieces — chairs and sofas that have been in families for generations, pieces with genuine history and irreplaceable frames. These require a different kind of attention: careful stripping that preserves original tacking rails, sympathetic spring and webbing repair that respects the original construction, and fabric selection that honors the piece's character while making it livable.

We've restored Victorian settees, mid-century modern lounge chairs, traditional wing backs, and everything in between for Neptune Beach families. If you have a piece that matters to you, bring it to us. We'll give you an honest assessment of what's possible — and a result that will keep it in the family for another generation.
