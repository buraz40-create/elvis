Jacksonville's Window Treatment Experts Since 1999

The right window treatment does more than block light or provide privacy. It controls the mood of every room it inhabits — the quality of light that filters through, the texture it adds to a wall, the way it frames a view or conceals one. For homeowners and commercial clients on the First Coast, finding the right window treatment solution requires expertise, access to premium products, and the skill to install them flawlessly.

Elvis Upholstery & Draperies has been providing custom window treatments in Jacksonville since 1999. Our team brings 27 years of experience, a 300+ product showroom, and professional installation to every project — from a single Roman shade in Atlantic Beach to a full-home window treatment program for a Ponte Vedra estate.

Our Window Treatment Range

Roman Shades

The most versatile of all soft window treatments. Roman shades are available in flat, relaxed, hobbled, and London styles, in any fabric from your selection or ours. When raised, they stack neatly at the top of the window; when lowered, they create a clean, tailored panel of fabric. Ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens, and offices.

Roller Shades & Solar Shades

Clean, contemporary, and highly functional. Solar shades filter glare while preserving views — particularly valuable for Jacksonville's coastal properties where views are an asset. We offer standard and motorized roller shade systems with a wide range of opacity levels.

Cellular & Honeycomb Shades

The most energy-efficient window treatment option. Cellular shades trap air in their honeycomb cells, providing insulation from both summer heat and winter cool — a particular value in Florida's climate. Available in single, double, and triple-cell configurations.

Wood Blinds & Faux-Wood Blinds

Timeless, adaptable, and practical. Real wood blinds offer warmth and natural character; faux-wood blinds provide the same aesthetic with superior humidity resistance — ideal for bathrooms and coastal properties.

Plantation Shutters

The quintessential Florida window treatment. Plantation shutters provide precise light control, excellent durability, and a clean architectural look that adds real value to a home. We supply and install wood and composite shutters throughout the First Coast.

Motorized & Smart Window Treatments

Motorized systems are no longer a luxury — they're a practical solution for high windows, large windows, and homeowners who value convenience. Our motorized systems integrate with smart home platforms including Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit.

The Elvis Upholstery Window Treatment Process

Every window treatment project begins with an in-home or showroom measurement consultation. We take precise measurements of every window, evaluate light exposure and privacy needs, discuss your aesthetic goals, and recommend the right treatment for each application. A detailed written estimate follows within 24 hours.

Once your selections are confirmed and materials are received, we schedule installation at your convenience. Our installation team handles all mounting, hardware, and final adjustment — leaving your windows exactly as you envisioned them.

Frequently Asked Questions: Window Treatments in Jacksonville

What types of window treatments does Elvis Upholstery offer in Jacksonville?
We offer a comprehensive range of custom window treatments including Roman shades, roller shades, cellular and honeycomb shades, wood and faux-wood blinds, plantation shutters, motorized shade systems, valances, cornices, and custom draperies. Every treatment is measured, fabricated or sourced, and installed by our team.
Are motorized window treatments available in Jacksonville?
Yes. We offer motorized and smart-home-compatible window treatment systems from leading manufacturers. Motorized treatments are ideal for large windows, high windows, and for clients who want the convenience of app or voice control. Our team handles all programming and integration.
How do I choose the right window treatment for my home?
The right window treatment depends on four factors: light control, privacy, insulation, and aesthetic. Our team will walk you through every option at our Atlantic Beach showroom — considering your window orientation, your room's function, and your design goals — before making a recommendation. There's no pressure, just expertise.
Do you install window treatments throughout Jacksonville?
Yes. We measure, supply, and install custom window treatments throughout Jacksonville, Atlantic Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, St. Augustine, and Amelia Island. Our installation team handles all hardware mounting and final adjustment.
Can you handle window treatments for large or unusually shaped windows?
Absolutely. Custom window treatments are the ideal solution for oversized, arched, angled, bay, or skylight windows that standard products cannot serve. We take precise measurements and fabricate or source treatments to exact specification.
