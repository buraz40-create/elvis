Jacksonville's Premier Custom Drapery Studio

The window is the eye of every room. It frames your view of the world outside, controls how natural light fills your space, and — when dressed with the right fabric — defines the entire character of a room. For Jacksonville homeowners, interior designers, and commercial clients who understand this, there's one name on the First Coast that has delivered extraordinary custom draperies for 27 years: Elvis Upholstery & Draperies.

From the sweeping floor-to-ceiling linen panels of a Ponte Vedra Beach estate to the sun-filtering Roman shades of a Jacksonville Beach condominium, we design and handcraft custom draperies in Jacksonville that are built around your specific windows, your specific aesthetic, and your specific life.

Why Choose Custom Draperies Over Ready-Made?

Off-the-shelf window treatments are designed to fit thousands of different windows — which means they fit none of them perfectly. Custom draperies are engineered for your exact window dimensions, your exact ceiling height, your exact hardware configuration. The difference is visible the moment you walk into a room.

Custom draperies also give you complete control over fabric selection. The fabric you choose determines everything: how the panel drapes, how it controls light, how it ages, and how it relates to every other element in your room. Our Atlantic Beach showroom carries over 300 carefully curated fabrics — and our team's expertise ensures you make the right choice every time.

Our Custom Drapery Process

In-Home or Showroom Consultation

Every custom drapery project begins with measurement and design. Whether you visit our Atlantic Beach showroom at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7 or we come to you, we take precise measurements of every window, discuss your vision for the space, and guide you through heading styles, fabric options, lining specifications, and hardware choices.

Fabric Selection from 300+ Options

We carry linen, silk, velvet, sheer, performance, blackout, and specialty fabrics in our showroom — with access to hundreds more through our trade accounts. We also work with customer's own material (COM). Visit our affiliated fabric store at our showroom for additional selection.

Handcrafted Construction

Every panel is cut, sewn, and finished by hand in our Atlantic Beach workshop. Seams are straight, headings are crisp, pattern repeats are matched across panels, and every inch of lining is applied with care. Hems are hand-stitched or machine-stitched to specification. The result is drapery that hangs with the weight and elegance of a couture garment.

Professional Installation

We offer professional installation across the First Coast. Our team handles all hardware mounting, rod placement, bracket installation, and final dressing — steam-pressing and adjusting each panel until it hangs perfectly. No guesswork, no sagging, no gaps.

Frequently Asked Questions: Custom Draperies in Jacksonville

How much do custom draperies cost in Jacksonville, FL?
Custom draperies from Elvis Upholstery are priced based on fabric selection, header style, lining, and window size. A single custom drapery panel typically starts from $200–$600+, with installation available. Contact us at (904) 242-8778 for a free, written estimate at our Atlantic Beach showroom.
What drapery heading styles do you offer?
We offer all standard and specialty heading styles including pinch pleat (two, three, and four finger), goblet pleat, inverted pleat, rod pocket, grommet, tab top, wave/ripple fold, and custom decorative headings. Each heading style creates a different visual effect and drape profile — our team will guide you through the right choice for your space.
Do you offer drapery lining options?
Yes. We offer unlined, privacy-lined, blackout-lined, and interlining options. Lining significantly improves drape, prolongs fabric life, and enhances insulation. For coastal homes on the First Coast, we also recommend UV-protective lining to prevent sun damage to your fabric investment.
Can you make draperies for unusually shaped or large windows?
Absolutely. Custom draperies are ideal for windows that can't be served by off-the-shelf products — floor-to-ceiling windows, cathedral windows, bay windows, angled windows, and arched windows. We measure every window precisely and cut every panel by hand.
Do you install custom draperies in Jacksonville and surrounding areas?
Yes. Elvis Upholstery provides drapery installation throughout Jacksonville, Atlantic Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, St. Augustine, and Amelia Island. We handle hardware selection, wall mounting, and final styling of your draperies as part of our full-service offering.
