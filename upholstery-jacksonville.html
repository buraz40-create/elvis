Our Full Range of Services for Jacksonville Clients

Furniture Reupholstery Jacksonville

From antique wingback chairs in Avondale bungalows to contemporary sectionals in Southside townhomes, we reupholster every style of residential furniture with precision. Every piece is stripped, structurally assessed, re-sprung or re-webbed as needed, foam-fitted, and finished in your chosen fabric. We match patterns, hand-stitch welts, and deliver pieces that exceed the quality of the originals.

Custom Draperies Jacksonville

Our custom draperies serve Jacksonville homes from Mandarin to Murray Hill. Pinch pleat, wave fold, goblet pleat, Roman shades — every header style, every fabric, every lining specification — made to measure and installed by our team. We bring a designer's eye to every window.

Commercial Upholstery Jacksonville

Jacksonville's restaurant scene, hotel industry, and corporate office market all require commercial upholstery that performs. We work with commercial-grade fabrics rated for 100,000+ double rubs, marine-grade vinyls, and fire-code-compliant materials to deliver seating and interiors that hold up under real daily use — and still look exceptional.

Custom Headboards & Bedding

We build custom headboards, upholstered bed frames, and canopy bed treatments for Jacksonville's most beautiful bedrooms. Tufted, channel, nailhead, arched — every style, every size, built from scratch in our Atlantic Beach workshop.

Window Treatments Jacksonville

From Roman shades to plantation shutters, cellular shades to motorized roller systems — we supply, fabricate, and install window treatments across Jacksonville. Every treatment is sized precisely to your windows and installed by our professional team.

Does Elvis Upholstery serve all of Jacksonville, FL?
Yes. We serve the entire Jacksonville metropolitan area, including Riverside, Avondale, San Marco, Mandarin, Southside, Ortega, Murray Hill, Nocatee, downtown Jacksonville, and all surrounding zip codes. We offer pickup and white-glove delivery throughout Jacksonville.
How far is Elvis Upholstery's showroom from Jacksonville?
Our Atlantic Beach showroom at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7 is approximately 15–20 minutes from downtown Jacksonville and Riverside, and within 30 minutes of most Jacksonville neighborhoods. We also offer in-home consultations throughout Jacksonville.
Can you reupholster antique furniture in Jacksonville?
Yes. We regularly reupholster antique and heirloom pieces for Jacksonville clients. Our team is experienced with period-appropriate techniques, spring systems, and fabric selections that honor the original character of older pieces while bringing them back to exceptional condition.
