Amelia Island occupies a singular place in Florida's coastal landscape. At the northernmost tip of Florida's Atlantic coast, it combines the unspoiled beauty of thirteen miles of pristine beach with a historically rich downtown, a world-class resort community, and a residential culture that prizes quality, authenticity, and the kind of craftsmanship that matches the island's exceptional setting. For 27 years, Elvis Upholstery & Draperies has served Amelia Island homeowners, resort properties, and design professionals with custom upholstery and drapery that belongs on this extraordinary island.

Working on Amelia Island is a privilege our team takes seriously. The homes here — from the Victorian beauties of Fernandina Beach's Centre Street to the oceanfront estates of Summer Beach and the elegant residences of Amelia Island Plantation — all reflect a standard of quality that requires an equally exceptional upholstery and drapery partner. We bring that partnership to every Amelia Island commission, treating each project as what it is: an investment in one of Florida's most beautiful places to live.

Custom Upholstery for Amelia Island's Luxury Homes

Amelia Island's residential character ranges from the ornate Victorian homes of Fernandina Beach's historic district to the contemporary oceanfront estates of Summer Beach and the resort-adjacent residences of Amelia Island Plantation. Each setting has its own requirements — and we meet all of them.

For historic Fernandina Beach homes, we apply the same period-sensitive approach we use for historic properties in St. Augustine: fabrics, trims, and techniques that honor the original architectural character while bringing pieces back to exceptional functional condition. For oceanfront and resort-adjacent properties, we specify Sunbrella and marine-grade materials that perform in Amelia Island's coastal environment — salt air, humidity, and strong UV — without sacrificing the aesthetic quality these homes demand.

For all Amelia Island clients, we bring the same fundamental commitment: handcrafted work, honest pricing, and the personal service of a studio that genuinely cares about every piece it creates.

Resort & Hospitality Upholstery for Amelia Island Properties

Amelia Island is home to some of Florida's finest resort properties — including the Omni Amelia Island Resort and a number of boutique luxury retreats. The hospitality industry on Amelia Island requires commercial upholstery that performs at the highest level, because guests at these properties expect the best.

Elvis Upholstery & Draperies provides commercial-grade upholstery for Amelia Island's hospitality properties using fire-code-compliant, high-abrasion-rated fabrics and vinyls. We've completed seating programs for resort dining rooms, spa reception areas, and guest suite headboard programs — all delivered to the standard that Amelia Island's reputation demands.

We offer flexible scheduling for commercial projects, including weekend and evening installation, to ensure business continuity for resort and hospitality clients during the renovation process.

Drapery & Window Treatments for Amelia Island

Amelia Island's homes have windows worth dressing well. Ocean views, marsh vistas, live oak canopies — these are views that deserve to be framed rather than obscured. Our custom drapery and window treatment service creates treatments that enhance Amelia Island's natural beauty rather than competing with it.

We design and install pinch pleat draperies, Roman shades, motorized roller systems, plantation shutters, and specialty treatments for windows of every shape and size. For ocean-facing rooms, we recommend UV-protective lining as standard — protecting your fabric investment from Amelia Island's strong coastal sun.
