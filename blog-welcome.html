Welcome to Our New Website

Published March 10, 2012  ·  Elvis Upholstery & Draperies  ·  ← Back to Blog

Welcome to the new Elvis Upholstery & Draperies website. After years of serving Jacksonville and the First Coast from our Atlantic Beach showroom, we've launched a refreshed online presence that better reflects the quality of work we deliver every day — and makes it easier for new and returning clients to find us, explore our services, and get in touch.

Who We Are

Elvis Upholstery & Draperies is a family-owned and operated custom upholstery and drapery studio based at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7 in Atlantic Beach, Florida. We have been serving the Jacksonville metropolitan area and its finest interior designers since 1999 — providing residential and commercial upholstery, custom draperies, window treatments, custom headboards and bedding, and professional upholstery supplies to licensed trade professionals.

We are fully licensed, insured, and bonded. Every piece we produce is built by hand, in our workshop, by our team. We do not outsource, we do not rush, and we do not deliver a piece until it meets our standard.

What You'll Find on This Site

Our new website is designed to give you a clear picture of what we do, how we do it, and who we are. You'll find dedicated pages for every service we offer — from furniture reupholstery and custom draperies to commercial upholstery and custom headboards. You'll also find our full project gallery, a detailed explanation of our manufacturing process, and information about our upholstery supply distribution for trade professionals.

Serving the First Coast

From our Atlantic Beach showroom, we serve clients throughout Jacksonville, Atlantic Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, St. Augustine, and Amelia Island. We offer in-home consultations, pickup, and white-glove delivery — bringing the same standard of care to every project, whether it's a single accent chair or a full commercial interior.

Get in Touch

The best way to begin any project is with a conversation. Call us at (904) 242-8778, email us at sales@elvisupholstery.com, or stop by our showroom at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7, Atlantic Beach, FL 32233. We're open Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Free estimates are provided on every project — no obligation, no pressure.

We look forward to working with you.

New to Elvis Upholstery? Start with our Services page for a full overview of what we offer, or browse our project gallery to see examples of our work across Jacksonville and the First Coast.
