About Elvis Upholstery & Draperies

Family-owned and operated · Atlantic Beach, FL · Serving the First Coast since 1999

Creating Furniture for You, Not Just for Your Home

Elvis Upholstery and Draperies uses only the finest products and supplies to construct your furniture, and we guarantee that our level of quality will last for many years to come. Thanks to our partnerships with top manufacturers and companies both local and across the United States, we are able to provide a vast variety of products to our clientele. We've worked with many large interior design companies in the Jacksonville area, providing us with a breadth of experience that allows us to serve every client at the highest level.

From a single reupholstered accent chair to a complete commercial interior fit-out, we approach every project with the same commitment to craftsmanship, material quality, and personal service. That consistency — built over 27 years on the First Coast — is what keeps our clients coming back and sending their friends.

Why Choose Elvis Upholstery?

At Elvis Upholstery & Draperies, we fully commit to every client, no matter the size of the job. We carefully work through each step of the design process with our clients to ensure that the final product is truly satisfying. The pride we take in our work will not allow us to settle for anything short of excellence.

Who We Are

Elvis Upholstery & Draperies is a family-owned and operated business that has been serving the Jacksonville population and its top interior designers since 1999. We offer residential and commercial upholstery designed to fit your style, window treatments and custom bedding with exclusive fabrics and hardware — all made with the care and precision that only a dedicated craft studio can deliver.

Our showroom at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7 in Atlantic Beach is open to the public Monday through Friday 9am–5pm and Saturday 10am–2pm. We invite you to stop by, browse our fabric collection, and meet the team. We look forward to working with you.

Our Commitment to Quality

We use only the finest products and supplies available — sourced through our direct relationships with leading fabric manufacturers, foam suppliers, and hardware distributors. Our process begins with understanding what you want and ends with delivering exactly that. Every seam is hand-finished. Every pattern is matched. Every piece is inspected before delivery. That is the Elvis Upholstery standard, and it has not changed in 27 years.

Whether you're looking for a subtle fabric refresh or a complete custom commission built from the frame up, we guarantee that any furniture, drapery, or window treatment we create will satisfy you and stand the test of time.
