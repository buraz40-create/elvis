Our Service Philosophy

Every service we offer is built on the same foundation: honest consultation, premium materials, and handcrafted execution. We don't cut corners, we don't rush production, and we don't deliver a piece until it meets our standard. That standard has been consistent for 27 years — and it is why the First Coast's top interior designers, homeowners, and commercial operators choose Elvis Upholstery & Draperies.

We offer over 100,000 fabric options through our showroom and trade accounts, with access to exclusive collections from leading manufacturers across the United States. Whether you visit our Atlantic Beach showroom or we come to you for an in-home consultation, we bring the same level of expertise and personal care to every project.
