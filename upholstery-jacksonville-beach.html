Upholstery & Drapery for Oceanfront & Coastal Living

Jacksonville Beach homes face a specific set of upholstery and drapery challenges that inland homes simply don't encounter. Salt air corrodes metal hardware and degrades lesser fabrics. UV from east-facing ocean views fades unprotected upholstery within months. Humidity finds its way into foam cores that weren't properly specified for a coastal climate.

We solve these problems at the specification stage — before a single yard of fabric is cut. For Jacksonville Beach clients, we routinely specify Sunbrella and other solution-dyed acrylics for any sun-exposed upholstery, UV-protective interlining for east and south-facing draperies, and high-density Dryfast foam for any cushions that might see moisture. The result is upholstery and drapery that performs as well in year ten as it does on day one.
