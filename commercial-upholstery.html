Commercial Upholstery Built for Real Use

The difference between residential and commercial upholstery isn't just scale — it's specification. Commercial environments demand fabrics and construction methods that can withstand thousands of uses, industrial cleaning protocols, and years of continuous operation without showing meaningful wear.

At Elvis Upholstery, every commercial project begins with the right material specification. We work exclusively with commercial-grade fabrics and vinyls rated for the specific demands of your environment:

  • Naugahyde marine-grade vinyl — antimicrobial, bleach-cleanable, TB-117 fire compliant, ideal for restaurant and hospitality seating
  • Crypton performance fabrics — stain-proof, moisture-resistant, rated for 100,000+ double rubs
  • Sunbrella contract fabrics — UV-resistant, solution-dyed, ideal for outdoor and semi-outdoor commercial seating
  • COM contract textiles — we work with designer-specified fabrics for luxury commercial environments

Frequently Asked Questions: Commercial Upholstery in Jacksonville

What types of commercial upholstery do you provide in Jacksonville?
Elvis Upholstery provides commercial upholstery for restaurants, bars, hotels, country clubs, medical offices, corporate offices, retail environments, and event venues throughout Jacksonville and the First Coast. We handle booth seating, banquettes, dining chairs, lounge chairs, headboards, and full interior fit-outs.
What commercial-grade fabrics do you use?
We use Naugahyde marine-grade vinyl, Crypton performance fabrics, COM contract-grade textiles, and Sunbrella solution-dyed acrylics — all rated for heavy commercial use and compliant with California TB-117 fire codes. Fabrics are selected for their abrasion rating (typically 100,000+ double rubs), stain resistance, and cleanability.
Can you complete commercial upholstery projects on a tight timeline?
Yes. We have completed full restaurant fit-outs over a single weekend to avoid disrupting business operations. Commercial timeline planning is part of every project estimate — we work around your schedule, not ours.
Do you handle commercial upholstery for restaurants in Jacksonville?
Absolutely. Restaurant upholstery is one of our most frequent commercial service areas. We handle everything from single booth replacements to full dining room fit-outs, including curved banquettes, custom dining chairs, bar stools, and host area seating. All materials are bleach-cleanable and fire-code compliant.
Do you serve commercial clients outside Jacksonville?
Yes. We serve commercial clients throughout Northeast Florida including Atlantic Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Ponte Vedra Beach, St. Augustine, and Amelia Island. For large commercial projects, we can also discuss projects further afield — contact us to discuss your specific needs at (904) 242-8778.
