Custom Upholstery & Drapery in St. Augustine, FL

Custom Upholstery & Drapery for America's Oldest City & St. Johns County

27 years on the First Coast Atlantic Beach Showroom Free Written Estimates Pickup & White-Glove Delivery

St. Augustine is unlike any other city in Florida. As America's oldest continuously inhabited European settlement, it carries a sense of history, character, and craftsmanship that permeates every corner — from the coquina walls of the Castillo de San Marcos to the narrow streets of the historic district, the Victorian homes of the bayfront, and the modern luxury developments of northern St. Johns County. Homes in St. Augustine demand furnishings that honor that character. Elvis Upholstery & Draperies is the First Coast studio that rises to that standard.

We serve St. Augustine and the broader St. Johns County area with the same handcrafted custom upholstery and drapery that has made us the First Coast's most trusted studio for 27 years. Whether you're restoring an antique piece in a historic bayfront home, furnishing a new construction in World Golf Village, or fitting out a boutique hotel in the historic district, our team delivers quality that belongs in St. Augustine's exceptional setting.

Custom Upholstery for St. Augustine's Historic Homes

St. Augustine's historic homes — Victorians, Spanish Colonial revivals, early-twentieth-century craftsmen — often contain furniture and architectural details that simply cannot be served by off-the-shelf upholstery solutions. A Victorian parlor sofa needs a fabric that's sympathetic to its period. A Spanish revival sitting room requires window treatments that honor its architectural vocabulary. A craftsman bungalow deserves upholstery that feels honest and enduring.

We bring 27 years of experience in period-appropriate and architecturally sensitive upholstery and drapery to St. Augustine's historic homes. We select fabrics, heading styles, and treatment approaches that belong in these spaces — and we execute them with the level of craft that historic St. Augustine deserves.

Commercial Upholstery for St. Augustine's Hospitality Industry

St. Augustine's tourism industry is one of the largest in Florida — and its hospitality businesses, from boutique bed and breakfasts in the historic district to full-service resort hotels, require commercial upholstery that performs at scale without sacrificing the character that makes St. Augustine special.

We work with St. Augustine hotels, restaurants, and hospitality venues on commercial upholstery programs that use fire-code-compliant, commercial-grade materials — while maintaining the aesthetic integrity of their interiors. We've furnished dining rooms in the historic district, lobby seating for boutique hotels, and restaurant booth programs for St. Augustine's culinary businesses. Our commercial team handles large projects efficiently, with weekend and after-hours scheduling to minimize disruption.

St. Johns County Residential Service

Beyond St. Augustine's historic core, St. Johns County has developed into one of Florida's most sought-after residential destinations. World Golf Village, Palencia, Nocatee, and the growing communities along the St. Johns River corridor are home to a new generation of discerning homeowners who want quality custom furnishings that match the quality of their homes.

Elvis Upholstery & Draperies serves these communities with in-home consultations, fabric selections from our 300+ swatch showroom, and white-glove delivery of finished pieces. Whether you're reupholstering a beloved sofa, commissioning a custom headboard, or dressing a wall of windows with custom draperies, we bring the same standard of care to every St. Johns County commission.

It's a city that understands the difference between something mass-produced and something made by hand — and it's a city that's willing to invest in quality that will last.
