Professional Upholstery Supplies at Trade Prices

In addition to our full upholstery and drapery service offering, Elvis Upholstery & Draperies distributes professional upholstery supplies to licensed business owners throughout the First Coast and Northeast Florida. Through our direct relationships with major manufacturers, we're able to offer a comprehensive inventory at competitive trade pricing.

Whether you're a professional upholsterer, an interior designer sourcing materials for a project, or a licensed business owner in need of a reliable supply partner, we have the inventory and the expertise to serve you. Call us at (904) 242-8778 for current pricing and availability.

Nailheads & Decorative Legs

We also stock an extensive range of nailhead trims and decorative furniture legs for professional use. Our nailhead selection covers standard and specialty finishes — antique brass, polished nickel, oil-rubbed bronze, and more — in a variety of sizes and spacing configurations. Our decorative leg selection includes turned, tapered, bun, and contemporary styles in multiple wood finishes and metal options.

For a full catalogue of our nailhead inventory and decorative leg selection, contact our team directly at (904) 242-8778 or email sales@elvisupholstery.com.
