Curved & Radius Seating

Curved built-ins require a frame built to a specific radius, foam cut to contour, and fabric pulled without puckers around the curve. This is skilled work â€” and it is one of our specialities. Whether the radius is tight (a circular banquette) or gentle (a curved window seat), we build the frame in-house and execute the upholstery to a fine-furniture standard.

Our Built-In Process

Site Measurement & Design

Every built-in project begins with a site visit. We measure the space, assess the substrate (wall framing, floor material, existing millwork), and discuss height, depth, fabric, and function. For commercial projects, we review floor plans and work with your general contractor or designer if needed.

Frame Construction

All frames are built in-house at our Atlantic Beach workshop from kiln-dried hardwood and premium MDF. We do not use flat-pack or prefabricated frames. This means we can accommodate non-standard dimensions, curved profiles, and unusual site conditions that off-the-shelf products cannot.

Foam & Padding

Seat foam is selected by density and ILD (firmness rating) based on the intended use â€” firmer for restaurant seating rated for continuous commercial use, softer for residential window seats. We use HR (High Resilience) foam, Dacron wrap, and in some applications, spring-assisted seat bases for maximum comfort and longevity.

Fabric & Finishing

Fabric is cut and sewn in-house, with seams, welt cords, tufting, and nailhead details executed to the same standard as our fine-furniture upholstery. We work with customer's own material (COM) and also carry an extensive fabric library in the showroom.

Installation

For residential built-ins, our team installs the finished piece and coordinates with any millwork, electrical, or finish carpentry. For commercial projects, we work around your operating schedule â€” many of our restaurant fit-outs are completed over a single weekend to avoid business disruption.

Frequently Asked Questions: Custom Built-In Seating in Jacksonville

What types of built-in seating do you build?
We build restaurant banquettes and booths, window seats, bay seating, bedroom benches, curved radius seating, and full commercial booth installations for residential and commercial clients throughout Jacksonville and the First Coast.
Do you build curved banquettes?
Yes — curved and radius seating is a specialty. We build the frame in-house to the exact radius required, cut foam to contour, and upholster with tight, pucker-free fabric around the curve.
What fabrics do you use for commercial built-ins?
For commercial projects we use Naugahyde marine-grade vinyl, Crypton performance fabrics, Sunbrella solution-dyed acrylics, and COM contract-grade textiles — all rated for heavy commercial use and California TB-117 fire-code compliant.
Can you complete a restaurant fit-out over a weekend?
Yes. We have completed full restaurant booth fit-outs over a single weekend to avoid disrupting operations. Commercial timeline planning is part of every project estimate — we work around your schedule, not ours.
Do you work with customer's own material (COM)?
Absolutely. We accept COM for all built-in upholstery projects. We can provide yardage estimates in advance so you order the correct amount.
