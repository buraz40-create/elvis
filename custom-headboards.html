Custom Headboards in Jacksonville, FL — Bespoke Bedrooms Built by Hand

27 years of Craft Atlantic Beach Showroom Free Written Estimates Serving the First Coast

The Bedroom Centerpiece — Built Exactly for You

The headboard is the most personal piece of furniture in your home. It's the first thing you see when you wake up and the last thing you see at night. It anchors the entire bedroom — setting the tone for every other furnishing, fabric, and finish in the space. And yet, for most people, the headboard is an afterthought: something that came with the bed, or something picked off a showroom floor from a finite collection of options.

At Elvis Upholstery & Draperies, we believe your headboard should be exactly what you want it to be — designed from scratch, built by hand, and crafted from a fabric that you love. We've been creating custom headboards in Jacksonville and across the First Coast for 27 years, and every piece we've built has been a genuine original.

From Sketch to Bedroom Centerpiece

Design Consultation

Bring us a photo, a sketch, a Pinterest board, or just a feeling. Our team at 1079 Atlantic Blvd #7 in Atlantic Beach will help you define the scale, shape, style, and fabric of your headboard — and mock up the dimensions against your room layout to ensure perfect proportion.

Frame Construction

Every headboard begins with a frame built in-house from kiln-dried hardwood or MDF, depending on the design. We cut every silhouette — whether a standard rectangle, a dramatic arch, or a custom curved profile — to precise specifications. The frame is then fitted with the appropriate foam specification for your headboard style.

Upholstery & Detail Work

This is where the craft lives. Tufting buttons are set at mathematically precise intervals. Channel stitching is sewn with perfectly consistent spacing. Nailhead trim is applied piece by piece, hammered or glued by hand into a flawless line. Welt cord seams are pressed and aligned. The fabric is pulled, stretched, and secured with the same tension across every inch of the surface.

Headboard Styles We Create

  • Button tufted — diamond and square grid patterns, shallow and deep tufting
  • Channel tufted — vertical, horizontal, and curved channel variations
  • Nailhead trim — individual nail, continuous banding, and decorative patterns
  • Panel headboards — clean, contemporary upholstered panels with welt cord detailing
  • Arched headboards — dramatic curved silhouettes, from gentle to bold
  • Wingback headboards — full wing extension, ideal for reading and lounging
  • Full upholstered bed frames — headboard, footboard, rails, and platform base
  • Canopy bed surrounds — custom drapery and canopy treatments integrated with the headboard

Fabric Store & Material Selection

Our Atlantic Beach showroom carries over 300 upholstery fabric swatches — and for headboards, the selection matters enormously. Performance velvet gives a rich, tactile luxury feel. Linen adds natural texture and casual elegance. Boucle is having a design moment and translates beautifully to a headboard. Leather and faux leather offer a clean, contemporary edge. Visit our showroom for our full fabric supply store, or call us at (904) 242-8778 to discuss your vision.

Frequently Asked Questions: Custom Headboards in Jacksonville

How much does a custom headboard cost in Jacksonville, FL?
Custom headboards from Elvis Upholstery are priced based on size, fabric selection, and design complexity. A standard king tufted headboard typically ranges from $800–$2,500+. Full upholstered bed frames (headboard, footboard, and rails) range from $2,000–$6,000+. Contact us at (904) 242-8778 for a free written estimate.
What headboard styles do you offer?
We create button tufted, diamond tufted, channel stitched (vertical and horizontal), nailhead trim, panel, arched, shaped, and fully custom silhouette headboards. We also build complete upholstered bed frames including platform bases, wingback styles, and canopy bed surrounds.
Can you match a specific design I've seen?
Yes. We regularly work from Pinterest images, interior design drawings, and client sketches. Bring us your reference image and we'll faithfully recreate it in the fabric of your choice — or improve on it. This is the advantage of truly custom work.
What fabrics work best for headboards?
Performance velvets, linen blends, and woven textiles are the most popular choices for headboards due to their durability and visual richness. Crypton and performance fabrics are ideal for households with pets or children. We'll guide you through the best options for your lifestyle at our Atlantic Beach showroom.
Do you make headboards for all bed sizes?
Yes. We build custom headboards for twin, full, queen, king, California king, and non-standard custom bed sizes. We also accommodate platform bed heights, wall-mounted headboards, and headboards designed to reach the ceiling for a dramatic architectural effect.
